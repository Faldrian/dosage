Dosage comic SmackJeeves/ItsLifeJim

DescriptionThis is a webcomic based on the real life drama and humorous events which happen to me and my two flatmates whilst at uni. It'll run the three years we're here together. Most of the events are true... although some are over dramatised for your amusement. The art style changes several time throughout and gets better each time. towards the middle of the second year it had full colour in every comic. So please bare with the bad to get to the good. Everyone in this comic is a real person! Enjoy! (Includes some guest comics, including ones by Chris himself... see if you can spot them!)
