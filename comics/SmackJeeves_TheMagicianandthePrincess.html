Dosage comic SmackJeeves/TheMagicianandthePrincess

DescriptionHere is my own take of Alice in Wonderland/Cinderella. This story begins with Alfie, falling into a crazy show, there he meets a bizarre set of characters, including a servant girl, Mary-Ann(daughter of a famous Magician). During an up-raw with the 'Red' Prince, Alfie rescues Mary-ann from being beheaded and escape, involving them both in the Red prince's interest... Alfie later discovers his dream was no illusion when familiar faces show up to hunt down the mysterious 'White Princess'; a possible childhood acquaintance. Also, being a threat himself to the Red Prince, Alfie learns he must now protect this White Princess around the clock, without knowing whom she really is... So with the assistance of his butler 'Jack' and The knave 'Trixie', he uses his mirror disguise 'Jasper' to stop them while he fools the public into thinking it's all a big act! Meanwhile, as Mary-ann holds hope in her father's promise, she finds she must inherit his magic and clever disguises in order to inspire and protect Alfie and her new friends. Where do you think all this will lead them into...? Follow Alfie and Mary-ann, into a world of magic. May the show begin!!
