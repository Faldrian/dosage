DescriptionThe story of an ordinary guy stuck in a never-ending acid trip. Tripp must learn to cope with his new psychotropically enhanced existence with the aid of his spirit guide, Edgar Allan Poe. This comic is not an endorsement of drug use, merely one guy's effort to deal with an accidental, perpetual hallucinatory life change.
